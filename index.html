Game Rules

OFFICIAL GAME RULES

  • 2 groups will compete head-to-head. Each group consists of exactly 6 members.
  • The core game consists of 6 questions in total.
  • Each question has 5 options on the board, each carrying different point values.
  • Each group has exactly 10 seconds to answer the question after ringing the bell.
  • Groups get one chance to answer. If incorrect, the turn immediately passes to the next group.
  • Play continues until all 5 answers are found, or we move on if both groups fail to answer.
  • Tie-Breaker: If the two teams are tied at the end, we will do a tie-breaker with one final question.
